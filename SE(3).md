# SE(3)

Special Euclidean Group

## Definition
The definition is at 1:29.28 in Ref[1]. <br>

$`SE(3)=\{(p, R): p \in \mathbb{R^3} \text{ and } R \in SO(3)\}`$ <br>
Action/Operation of $`g \in SE(3)`$ on $`{\mathbb{R^3}}`$ is $`g(q) = p + Rq \text{ for } q \in \mathbb{R^3}`$.
