# Rotation Matrics

## SO(3) Group

## Properties
Let $`R`$ be a rotation matrix, then the following properties hold:

### Inverse
$`R^{-1}=R^T \in SO(3)`$

### Closure
$`R_{1}R_{2} \in SO(3)`$

### Associative
$`(R_{1}R_{2})R_{3}=R_{1}(R_{2}R_{3})`$

### Not commutative
$`R_{1}R_{2} \neq R_{2}R_{1}`$

### Vector length preservation
$`x \in \mathbb{R}^3, \|Rx\|=\|x\|`$
